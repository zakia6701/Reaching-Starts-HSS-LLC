About Us

At Reaching Starts HSS LLC, we are dedicated to providing high-quality services to the homeless community. Our team of professionals is committed to helping individuals and families who are experiencing homelessness to find safe and secure housing, as well as access to essential resources and support services. We believe that every person deserves a safe place to call home, and we are committed to working tirelessly to make this a reality for everyone in our community.

Services

Emergency Shelter

If needed, we offer to bridge our clients with one of our multiple partnered shelters in and around the twin cities in order to help our clients fulfil any dire emergency needs.

Transitional Housing

Our transitional housing programs provide a stable and supportive environment for individuals and families as they transition from homelessness to permanent housing.

Sustaining Services

Our sustaining services provide clients with resources and direct help keeping their new home.

Outreach and Advocacy

We are committed to advocating for the rights and needs of the homeless community.
